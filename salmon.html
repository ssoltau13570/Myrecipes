Baked Salmon with Roasted Charred Veggie Medley

A perfectly timed sheet-pan dinner — the veggies get a head start so everything comes out at once. Flaky salmon and beautifully charred vegetables every time.

Serves4
Prep5 min

Notes
The veggie head start is the whole secret here — by the time everything is done they will have had about 37 minutes total, which is perfect for that caramelized char. Keep an eye on the salmon in the final blast — thinner fillets may be done a minute or two early. Great with a squeeze of fresh lemon at the table.
