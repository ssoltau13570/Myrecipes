My Recipe Collection
A personal digital cookbook built to make home cooking more accessible, organized, and enjoyable. Live at ssoltau13570.github.io/Myrecipes — no apps, no subscriptions, no ads. Just food.

Why this exists
Too many recipe sites bury the actual recipe under walls of ads and endless backstory. This archive exists to cut through all of that — a clean, personal, always-growing collection of real home recipes that are easy to find, easy to read, and easy to cook from on any device.
Built with accessibility in mind for people managing health conditions, busy schedules, low energy days, and tight budgets. Every recipe is tagged and organized so you can find what works for your life, not just what's trending.

How to use it
Browsing
Recipes are organized into categories — Vegetarian, Fish, Soups/Salads/Sandwiches, Drinks, Meat Mains, Breads & Pizza, Baking, and Desserts. Each category has a collapsible ingredient navigator so you can jump directly to what you're looking for.
Searching
Use the search bar at the top to find recipes by name or ingredient. Results update instantly as you type.
Filtering
Tap the Filter button to open a three-row filter drawer organized by Dietary needs, Lifestyle, and Cuisine. Mix and match filters to find exactly what fits your day.
Icons at a glance
Every recipe card shows icons so you can scan quickly without reading every card:

⏱ Under 30 minutes total
🥄 Low effort / ADHD friendly — slow cooker, one pan, minimal active steps
❄️ Freezer friendly
📅 Great for meal prep
🌱 Uses garden fresh ingredients
🎮 Great for game night or get-togethers
🚲 Pack and bike friendly

The Garden section
Scroll to the bottom or tap the Garden button to browse by what's growing. Select one or more ingredients, choose Any (recipes using at least one) or All (recipes using every selected ingredient), then tap Show Recipes to filter the collection.
Seasonal strip
The green bar just below the hero highlights what's currently being planted or harvested. Tap any ingredient for an instant search.
Meal planning with AI
Tap the Meal Plan button for a copyable template. Paste it into Claude.ai, describe your week — energy level, health needs, budget, workouts — and Claude will pull from this library to build a personalized weekly plan with a consolidated grocery list.
