# My Personal Recipe Archive — Built with Claude

A fully organized, searchable digital cookbook built collaboratively with Claude AI. Live at ssoltau13570.github.io/Myrecipes — no apps, no subscriptions, works on any device.

## What's inside
- 20 recipes across 4 categories — Vegetarian, Fish, Meat Mains, and Baking & Desserts
- Every recipe includes:
  - Adjustable servings — scale any recipe up or down and every ingredient amount updates instantly
  - Countdown timers with an audio chime alarm built into each timed step
  - Step-by-step instructions laid out for easy cooking

## How it's organized
- Filtered by dietary tags: Vegetarian, Vegan, Gluten Free, GERD Friendly, PCOS Friendly, and more
- Filtered by style: Quick, Slow Cooker, Comfort Food, Baking, Favorites
- Cards alphabetized A-Z within each category
- Letter navigation sorted by main ingredient for fast scrolling
- At-a-glance icons on every card:
  - Under 30 minutes total
  - Low effort / ADHD friendly (slow cooker, one pan, minimal active steps)
  - Freezer friendly
  - Great for meal prep

## AI-powered meal planning
Because the archive is live on the web, you can share the URL with Claude and ask it to help you plan your week based on your energy level, health goals, budget, or what's already in your fridge. Claude can cross-reference ingredients across recipes to keep your grocery list short, suggest recipe modifications for specific health needs, and put together a full week plan with a consolidated shopping list in minutes.
