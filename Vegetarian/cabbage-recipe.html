✏️ Personal Notes
Braise for 15 minutes for wedges that hold their shape better. Raw red pepper works great for the sauce — add a pinch of smoked paprika to mimic that roasted flavor. Skip the serrano chile for GERD. Go easy on the lime. Leftovers are great the next day for lunch — reheat and add a soft boiled egg on top!
